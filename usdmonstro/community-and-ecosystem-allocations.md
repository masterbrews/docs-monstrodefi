# Community & Ecosystem Allocations

The Community & Ecosystem allocation represents Monstro’s long-term commitment to honoring past products, acquired brands, and recovery initiatives. These tokens convert legacy positions into $MONSTRO stakes, unifying the entire history of Monstro under one system.

***

## **Allocation Breakdown (208M MONSTRO)**

| Community / Brand | Allocation | Notes                                                                   |
| ----------------- | ---------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Monstro           | 97,000,000 | Based on each holder’s calculated conversion value (varies by product). |
| Lazarus Pit       | 84,000,000 | Allocated at **7 MONSTRO per share**.                                   |
| Whelps            | 15,000,000 | Distributed using a **trait-based rarity algorithm**.                   |
| Heroes & Villains | 9,000,000  | Distributed using a **trait-based rarity algorithm**.                   |
| MasterBrews       | 3,000,000  | Based on each wallet’s **virtual BREW token balance**.                  |

***

## **Purpose of These Allocations**

This 200M supply segment is designed to:

* Make legacy holders whole
* Consolidate all past Monstro-related products
* Bring acquired communities into the unified $MONSTRO system
* Provide continuity and fairness for long-time supporters
* Maintain transparent distribution rules across all brands

Every community uses a conversion method tailored to how that product originally functioned.

***

## **How Conversion Works**

These allocations become available through the **Auto-Stake** system:

* Holders claim their allocation by pressing **Claim Allocation** after launch.
* Once claimed, the MONSTRO allocation is automatically **staked into the main contract** and behaves just like any regular stake.
* Claim windows vary by community (**6 or 12 months**).
* Unclaimed amounts follow the standard expiry and redistribution rules (burn / treasury / emission refill).

_Detailed expiry mechanics are covered on the_ [_Auto-Stake Allocations_](auto-stake-allocations.md) _page._

***

## **How These Tokens Behave Once Claimed**

After activation, all converted allocations function exactly like any other staked MONSTRO:

* Earn emissions
* Contribute to tier status (Vault, Fortress, Kingdom)
* Count toward DAO governance voting
* Follow the same penalty decay rules
* Can be compounded or partially withdrawn
* Are transferable if the stake is transferred

Legacy holders gain **full ecosystem rights** with no restrictions or separate rules.

***

## **Why This Matters**

This system accomplishes three core goals:

1. **Fairness & Restoration**\
   Legacy participants receive a modern, liquid, upgrade-path version of their historical value.
2. **Unified Ecosystem**\
   All past brands and products now operate under a single token and staking structure.
3. **Active Participation Over Dead Value**\
   Instead of permanently illiquid NFTs or abandoned plans, holders receive usable MONSTRO that powers the new ecosystem.
