# DAO

Monstro DAO is the governing body of the $MONSTRO ecosystem. It oversees the protocol treasury, manages long-term decisions, and safeguards the integrity of the token and its emissions. The DAO now operates through a formally registered legal wrapper (Monstro DAO LLC, Republic of the Marshall Islands) while continuing to expand its on-chain governance infrastructure. Its structure ensures that control of the protocol rests with the community—not any centralized team.

The DAO will be powered by staked $MONSTRO, with voting rights distributed proportionally to long-term participants. Over time, governance will expand to include proposals, parameter changes, treasury decisions, and the election of Council members who help steward the ecosystem.
