# Staking

## Staking $MONSTRO

The $MONSTRO staking system is a core component of the Monstro ecosystem.\
It is fully on-chain, non-custodial, and designed to reward long-term participation while remaining transparent and flexible.

Staking allows $MONSTRO holders to earn emissions, unlock tier-based bonuses, and align with the long-term growth of the protocol without surrendering custody of their tokens.

This section serves as a central hub for all staking-related actions and information.

***

## Staking Hub

● [**Creating a Stake**](user-guide/staking/creating-a-stake)\
Create your first stake and begin earning rewards.

● [**Claiming Rewards**](user-guide/staking/claiming-rewards)\
Withdraw earned $MONSTRO rewards to your wallet without affecting your stake.

● [**Compounding Rewards**](user-guide/staking/compounding-rewards)\
Automatically add earned rewards back into your stake to grow faster over time.

● [**Adding to Your Stake**](user-guide/staking/adding-to-your-stake)\
Increase your existing stake to earn more rewards and unlock higher tiers.

● [**Transferring Your Stake**](user-guide/staking/transferring-your-stake)\
Move your entire stake to another wallet address in a single transaction.

● [**Gifting a Stake**](user-guide/staking/gifting-a-stake)\
Create a staked position for another wallet by gifting $MONSTRO directly into a new stake.

● [**Unstaking**](user-guide/staking/unstaking)\
Withdraw part or all of your staked $MONSTRO with full visibility into penalties and outcomes.
