# Unstaking

## Understanding unstaking

When you unstake $MONSTRO, a **penalty may apply** depending on how long your tokens have been staked.

Your current penalty:

* Is shown directly on the staking page
* **Decays continuously over time**
* Applies **only to the tokens you unstake**, not your entire stake

Any **pending rewards** are automatically claimed **without penalty** when you unstake.

## Step 1: Open the unstake modal

From the **Stake** page, click **Unstake** in the **My Stake** section.

The unstake modal will display:

* Your available unstake amount
* A real-time penalty breakdown
* The exact amount you will receive

![Image](https://3282321646-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FYvsc7QM1Xh6lTiwPxIVV%2Fuploads%2FULF3Muau5oaLDm6CEizd%2Funstake-2.png?alt=media&#x26;token=e2193070-cfb8-4c5a-8cfe-9d85f8c8920b)

***

## Step 2: Review the penalty breakdown

As you enter the amount to unstake, the penalty breakdown updates automatically.

You will see:

* **Unlock Amount** – the amount you chose to unstake
* **Penalty** – the portion deducted based on your current penalty rate
* **You’ll Receive** – the final amount sent to your wallet

This ensures there are no surprises before confirming.

***

## Step 3: Confirm unstaking

Click **Confirm Unstake** and approve the transaction in your wallet. A small amount of **ETH on Base** is required for gas.

Once confirmed, the unstaked tokens are sent to your wallet immediately.

![Image](https://3282321646-files.gitbook.io/~/files/v0/b/gitbook-x-prod.appspot.com/o/spaces%2FYvsc7QM1Xh6lTiwPxIVV%2Fuploads%2Fq0yejiL9rhssEWhlS9Yt%2Funstake-3.png?alt=media&#x26;token=ce04f246-3aca-4378-b194-d4908bfad64c)

***

## How penalties are used

Any unstaking penalty is distributed as follows:

* **50% is permanently burned**, reducing total supply
* **25% is used to refill emissions**, supporting long-term rewards
* **25% goes to the DAO treasury**, strengthening community governance

This ensures penalties benefit the ecosystem rather than a single party.

***

## Important notes

* You can **partially or fully unstake** at any time
* Penalties **decay continuously** the longer you remain staked
* Pending rewards are **never penalized**
* Unstaking affects only the amount you choose to withdraw

***

## Closing note

Unstaking is always available, with clear and transparent outcomes shown before you confirm. For the best results, many users choose to wait until penalties have significantly decayed.
