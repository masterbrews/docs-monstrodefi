# Socials

Stay connected with the Monstro ecosystem through our active community platforms. Get updates, ask questions, share ideas, and participate in discussions as the project evolves.

## **Our Social Platforms**

**●** [**Discord**](https://discord.gg/monstrodefi)\
The main hub for community chat, announcements, support, and discussion.

**●** [**Telegram**](https://t.me/MonstroPortal)\
Quick updates, casual discussion, and a convenient option for mobile users.

**●** [**X (Twitter)**](https://x.com/monstrodefi)\
Project news, launch updates, governance announcements, and ecosystem insights.

**●** [**Reddit**](https://www.reddit.com/r/MonstroDefi/)\
Long-form updates, official announcements, governance discussion, and canonical project information.

These are the only official Monstro DeFi communication channels.\
Join us and be part of a growing, engaged community.
