# Auto-Stake Allocations

## **Who Qualifies**

Users from **past Monstro products and brands** may receive a one-time auto-stake allocation at launch, including:

* Lazarus Pit
* Whelps
* Heroes & Villains
* MasterBrews
* Monstro product sets (Farmz, Degenz, Poolz, Golemz, Pokz)

These allocations convert legacy investments into MONSTRO tokens.

***

## **How Conversion Works**

Each eligible user receives a predetermined MONSTRO allocation based on the **original investment value** of their past Monstro NFTs.

#### Conversion Rates (Monstro-native products)

| Product        | Conversion Rate |
| -------------- | --------------- |
| Farmz / Degenz | 100%            |
| Poolz          | 100%            |
| Golemz         | 125%            |
| Pokz           | 50%             |

#### Notes

* These percentages apply to **Monstro-created** products only.
* Lazarus Pit holders receive 22% of their Optimus loss as value.
* Acquired brands have **their own trait- and rarity-based formulas**.

***

## **Claiming Your Allocation**

Auto-stake allocations do **not** activate automatically.

At or after launch, users must:

#### ✔ Press **“Claim Allocation”** on the dApp

This verifies eligibility (via Merkle proof) and creates your stake on-chain.

Once claimed:

* Your MONSTRO tokens are immediately **auto-staked**
* Your stake begins accruing emissions
* You receive full DAO voting rights
* Your position behaves exactly like any normal stake

There is **no difference** between auto-stakers and regular stakers — **same emissions, same tiers, same mechanics, same contract.**

***

## **Expiry Windows**

Auto-stake allocations have fixed claim windows.

If not claimed before expiry, the tokens are redistributed (burn + treasury + refill).

#### **6-Month Claim Window**

Applies to:

* Whelps
* HV
* MasterBrews

#### **12-Month Claim Window**

Applies to:

* Lazarus Pit
* Monstro products

#### After Expiry

Unclaimed tokens are moved to an **Expired Allocation Pool**, then split as:

* 50% burned
* 25% to the DAO treasury
* 25% added back to the emissions pool

***

## **Your Stake After Claiming**

Once claimed, your auto-stake:

* Earns $MONSTRO emissions continuously
* Grants tier bonuses if above thresholds
* Can be compounded, withdrawn, or transferred
* Has the same 2-year decreasing penalty
* Provides full DAO voting power
* Is fully compatible with all future ecosystem utilities

In short:

Auto-staked positions are first-class MONSTRO stakes with no special rules or restrictions.

***

## **Summary**

* Past NFT holders convert their historical investments into MONSTRO tokens
* Claiming activates an auto-stake identical to normal staking
* Conversion rates vary by product
* 6-month or 12-month claim windows apply
* Unclaimed allocations are eventually burned / redistributed
* Once claimed, users participate fully in the new ecosystem
