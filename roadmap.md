# Roadmap

> Live since January 27, 2026. Emissions active. DAO legally formed.

This roadmap outlines the major milestones guiding the rollout of the $MONSTRO token, the DAO transition, and ecosystem expansion. It is intentionally concise, execution-focused, and adaptable as governance evolves.

***

## ✅ Foundation + Launch (Completed)

* Contracts deployed and audits published
* MONSTRO live on Alien Base and Aerodrome
* Staking and emissions active
* Auto-stake claiming live
* DAO multisigs established and all legacy assets consolidated
* DAO legal wrapper finalized and published
* Governance documentation released
* Initial exchange and analytics listings secured

***

## 🔄 Current Focus — Strengthening the Core

* Liquidity strategy refinement across pools
* Active emissions monitoring and parameter tuning
* Expanded exchange and analytics visibility
* Ongoing community growth and education

***

## 🚀 Ecosystem Expansion (Q1–Q2)

* First formal DAO proposal and vote
* Launch of Monstro Labs’ first revenue product, Based.Loans
* Begin routing protocol revenue to the treasury
* Strategic partnerships within the Base ecosystem
* Evaluate CEX opportunities if liquidity and volume justify

***

## 🔮 Long-Term Development

* Additional Monstro Labs revenue products
* Treasury growth and capital deployment strategy
* Expanded governance capabilities
* Cross-product integrations and automation
* Sustainable deflation from protocol activity

***

## **A Living Roadmap**

This roadmap will evolve as milestones are completed and new opportunities emerge. Governance and market conditions will guide future adjustments.
