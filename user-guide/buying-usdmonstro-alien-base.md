# Buying $MONSTRO (Alien Base)

The recommended way to get started is directly from the Monstro website, which links to Alien Base with the correct swap pair pre-selected.

> **Note**\
> The steps below use Alien Base’s swap interface. Screenshots are provided as examples and may vary slightly once trading is live.

***

## Step 1: Open Alien Base from the Monstro site

Visit the [Monstro website](https://monstrodefi.com/) in your browser and click **Trade on Alien Base** on the homepage.

This will open Alien Base in a new tab with the $MONSTRO swap pair already selected, helping ensure you are interacting with the correct token.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/buy-1.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## Step 2: Connect your wallet on Alien Base

On Alien Base, click **Connect wallet** and approve the connection in your wallet.

Once connected, your wallet address and balances will be visible in the swap interface.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/buy-2.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## Step 3: Confirm the swap pair

The swap interface should already be configured to swap into **$MONSTRO**.

Confirm that:

* The token you are swapping **from** (for example, USDC) is correct
* The token you are swapping **to** is **MONSTRO**

If needed, you can change the input token using the token selector.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/buy-3.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## Step 4: Enter the amount to swap

Enter the amount of the input token you wish to swap.

The interface will estimate the amount you will receive based on current market conditions.

> **Pre-launch note**\
> Before trading is live, $MONSTRO may not display price data or may appear as “searching for price.”\
> Example screenshots may show a different token pair (such as USDC → ALB) for illustration purposes only.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/buy-4.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## Step 5: Preview the swap

After entering the amount, click **Preview swap** to review the transaction details before it is submitted.

This opens a confirmation panel showing the estimated output, slippage tolerance, and other swap details.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/buy-5.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## Step 6: Review the swap details

The confirmation panel displays a summary of the transaction, including:

* The amount you will pay
* The estimated amount you will receive
* Slippage tolerance
* Minimum amount received
* Fees and price impact

Review these details carefully before proceeding.

> **Note**\
> The output amount shown is an estimate. If price conditions change beyond your slippage tolerance, the transaction will automatically revert.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/buy-6.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## Step 7: Confirm the swap

If everything looks correct, confirm the swap and approve the transaction in your wallet.

Depending on market conditions, the confirmation button text may vary (for example, **Confirm** or **Swap Anyway**). This is normal and simply reflects additional confirmation due to price impact or volatility.

Once the transaction is confirmed on-chain, $MONSTRO will appear in your wallet balance.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/buy-7.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

### Additional notes

* Purchasing $MONSTRO does **not** stake your tokens. Tokens remain liquid in your wallet until you choose to stake them.
* To swap out of $MONSTRO, simply reverse the swap direction using the same interface.
* If you do not see $MONSTRO in your wallet after the swap, you may need to add the token manually using the official contract address shown on the Monstro site.
