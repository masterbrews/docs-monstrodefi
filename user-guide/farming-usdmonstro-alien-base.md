# Farming $MONSTRO (Alien Base)

This guide explains how to farm $MONSTRO on Alien Base by adding liquidity to the $MONSTRO vault.

When you add liquidity, your position is automatically staked in the farm, allowing you to earn both trading fees and farm rewards without any additional steps.

> **Pre-launch note**\
> Some screenshots in this guide may show a different token pair for illustration purposes. The interface and steps are the same and will apply to the $MONSTRO vault once liquidity is live.

***

## Step 1: Open the Add Liquidity page on Alien Base from the Monstro site

Visit the Monstro website in your browser and scroll down to the **Token Stats** section. Click **Add Liquidity**.

This will open Alien Base’s liquidity interface with the $MONSTRO / USDC pair pre-selected, helping ensure you are interacting with the correct pool.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/liq-1.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## Step 2: Connect your wallet on Alien Base

Once you are on Alien Base, click **Connect** in the top-right corner and approve the connection in your wallet.

You must be connected before you can view pools or add liquidity.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/liq-2.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## Step 3: Add liquidity from the vault page

After connecting your wallet, you can add liquidity from the vault page.

Click **Add liquidity** to begin supplying tokens to the vault.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/liq-3.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## Step 4: Choose Zap and enter the amount

After clicking **Add liquidity**, the add liquidity modal will open.

This vault uses a managed price range and includes a farm. Liquidity added here will be **automatically staked** after deposit.

Make sure **Zap (Any token)** is selected. This allows you to add liquidity using a single token.

Select the token you want to supply and enter the amount.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/liq-4 (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

> **Note**\
> You do not need to supply both tokens when using Zap.

***

## Step 5: Preview the Zap

After entering the amount, click **Preview ZAP**.

This opens a summary showing how your tokens will be swapped, added as liquidity, and staked in the farm.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/liq-5.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## Step 6: Review and confirm the Zap

Review the details shown, including:

* Estimated token amounts
* Price range (managed by the vault)
* Auto-staking status

If everything looks correct, click **Confirm ZAP**.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/liq-6.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## Step 7: Approve and sign transactions

Depending on your wallet, you may be asked to confirm **multiple transactions**.

Some wallets support batch transactions and may complete this in a single approval.\
Most wallets will guide you through several steps, such as:

1. Swap tokens
2. Approve token(s)
3. Add liquidity
4. Approve LP token
5. Stake in farm

This is normal. Simply approve each step as prompted by your wallet.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/liq-7.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

> **Note**\
> If your wallet does not support batch transactions, you will need to confirm each step individually. This does not change the outcome.

***

## Step 8: Wait for completion

As each transaction completes, the progress indicator will advance automatically.

Do not close the window while steps are still in progress.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/liq-8.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## Step 9: Liquidity added and staked

Once all steps are completed, you will see a confirmation screen indicating the process is complete.

Your liquidity has now been:

* Added to the vault
* Automatically staked in the farm

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/liq-9.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## After completion: View your position

After clicking **Done**, reload the page if your position does not appear immediately.

Once the page refreshes, open the **My Positions** tab to view your liquidity position and farm status.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/liq-10.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

***

## Understanding what you’re seeing

You may see **two rows** for the same vault. This is normal.

* One row represents your **liquidity position**
* The other represents your **staked position in the farm**

Because this vault includes a farm, liquidity added through Zap is automatically staked.

***

## Why it may show a small amount “available to stake”

You may see a small amount listed as available to stake. This is typically leftover dust from the Zap process and does not require any action unless you want to stake it.

***

## About rewards and harvesting

Rewards accrue automatically over time. If rewards are available, they can be harvested from this page.
